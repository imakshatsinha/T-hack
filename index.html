#developersToTeachersToStudents

What is DESK ?


Desk is feature-rich platform for students and teachers. Vision for Desk is to arrange a medium where teachers can organize classes for students and conduct proctored tests for students Here teacher gets ease-in platform to organize test and students ease-in platform to perform test!

What all Desk can do ?

Creates online test with proctored feature!.
Drag and drop makes it easy to create the test module.
Enhanced and eye-popping interface and experience.
We call it as free test-maker. Open Sourced for you all!
